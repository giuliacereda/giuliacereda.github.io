Open Science

I am committed to ensuring that my research is reproducible, transparent, and accessible. This page collects open-access versions of my publications, along with code and datasets supporting my work in forensic statistics, Bayesian modelling, and biomedical applications. Where direct sharing is restricted by copyright or ethical regulations, metadata and author-accepted versions are provided to facilitate responsible reuse.

All code supporting my research is available in open repositories under permissive licenses.

📊 Data Sharing

Where ethically and legally possible (e.g., COVID-19 modelling, simulation studies in forensic genetics), I share datasets or synthetic replicas in open repositories to ensure reproducibility. For sensitive data (e.g., forensic genetics, ancestry inference), anonymised or simulated datasets and metadata are provided in compliance with GDPR and national regulations.

📢 Preprints and Early Dissemination

I disseminate research outputs through preprint servers and conference proceedings to ensure early access to results and foster community feedback.

🤝 Community and Ethical Engagement

I actively organize and participate in interdisciplinary workshops addressing transparency, ethics, and data accessibility in forensic and biomedical statistics.
